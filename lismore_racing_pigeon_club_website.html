Welcome

Welcome to the Lismore Racing Pigeon Club. We are a local community of pigeon racing enthusiasts dedicated to the sport, camaraderie, and the thrill of competition.

About Us

Our club brings together experienced flyers and newcomers alike. We organise regular races, training, and social events. Whether you’re a seasoned racer or just curious, you’re welcome here.

Getting Started

New to pigeon racing? We can help you get started with advice on loft setup, bird care, and training. Reach out and we’ll guide you through your first steps in the sport.
